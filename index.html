About Sealatte Cafe

Welcome to our world of cafe where we serve the best combination of sweetness, bitterness and saltiness that comes from our pastries, coffees and beach sensation. Let's enjoy our premium taste of pastries and beverages with the best scenery and fresh air that you could never get from any other cafe.

Do come to our cafe for the best journey and experience!

P.S: "See you guys later at sealatte <3"

Dive Into Our Journey

Established on 2023 by four bestfriends who came from different background due to their similar hobby which is cafe hunting. Their main goal is to serve a balance flavour of bitter coffees with sweetness of pastries alongside the splash of saltiness from the sea that represents the sweetnees and bitterness of life.
